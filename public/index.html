Our Story

Equipment That Powers Better Outcomes

MediForma was founded with a single mission: equip healthcare environments with durable, hygienic stainless steel furniture built to the highest clinical standards. Every cabinet, trolley, and accessory we supply is manufactured from surgical-grade steel and finished to resist corrosion, bacteria, and the rigours of daily sterilisation.

From a single instrument table for a GP practice to a fully outfitted surgical suite, we configure and deliver solutions to every scale of facility.
